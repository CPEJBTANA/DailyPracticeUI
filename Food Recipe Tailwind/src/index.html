ENOKI BURGER

The Best Burger in Town

Recipe by: Lanz Toledo

★ ★ ★ ★

Instructions:

  • Mix: Combine 200g ground beef (or plant-based), 200g chopped enoki mushrooms, 1 minced garlic clove, 1/2 chopped onion, 1 egg, 1/4 cup breadcrumbs, salt, and pepper.
  • Form: Shape into patties.
  • Cook: Sauté in a skillet over medium heat for 4-5 minutes per side.
  • Assemble: Place patties on buns, add lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
  • Serve: Enjoy!
